  • Why do we need Bible Stars TV Show?

    In a world where the allure of secular entertainment often overshadows the principles of our faith, the need for shows like Bible Stars has never been more critical. Our youth are bombarded with messages that lead them away from God's truth, and it's time we reclaim the airwaves for His glory. Bible Stars is not just a show; it's a divine mandate entrusted to a young preacher of the gospel; Pastor Bectta Eferobome by the Almighty Himself. Through this platform, we can inspire a generation to rediscover the beauty and richness of God's Word.

  • The Story Behind Bible Stars: The Vision

    Bible Stars Reality Show was conceived during one of my yearly retreats—a personal fasting and prayer regimen from 6 am to 6 pm, spanning 93 days (October 1st to December 31st) every year. In 1999, during one of these retreats, God began revealing this vision to me. However, it took two years for me to gain clarity, though only about 15%. Initially, I struggled with God as my personal aspirations were quite different.

    I envisioned myself as a preacher, a crusade evangelist traversing countries to spread the gospel. Not being a social person, let alone interested in television, I pleaded with God to assign this mission to someone else. Like Jonah, I resisted and pursued my own endeavors, but God persisted. Various signs indicated the importance of this vision, including the failure of my desired business and ministry ventures.

    In 2009, despite my reservations, we conducted a small-scale trial in Ilorin, Kwara state, to test the concept's acceptance. Approximately 250 participants showed interest, and the grand finale garnered significant attendance. The grand prize back then was 50,000 Naira with a television set, the 2nd runner-up received a 43-inch television, and the third-place winner received a small generator, while other contestants received consolation prizes. Despite its success, I retreated from the project once more due to my lack of enthusiasm.

    However, during a trip to Pennsylvania, United States, I experienced a significant encounter. I woke up feeling paralyzed on the left side of my body, initially thinking it was a demonic attack. However, God revealed that it was a consequence of my stubbornness—ignoring His call. He made it clear that this was His final warning.Recognizing the significance of the project, I returned to Nigeria and assembled a team.

    Despite numerous setbacks and failures, we persisted with renewed passion and commitment. Fast forward to the present, and after three years of dedicated effort, we successfully concluded the eviction show of Bible Stars Reality Show Season 1 and are gearing up for the main event.

    One vision that holds me steadfast to this project is an image of God shedding tears as His people abandoned His word. This reflects the prevalent hypocrisy and superficial Christian life prevalent today. Christianity is not just about denominational affiliation; it's a supernatural experience and lifestyle with God—a reality we aim to showcase with the Bible Stars Reality Show.

    The journey continues, driven by a divine mandate. Bible Stars Reality Show is more than just a project; it's God's own endeavor, aimed at bringing His people back to Him. All glory to God for the journey so far, and may His name be praised for the journey ahead.

    -Pastor Eferobome Bectta

  • The Show

    The journey began with auditions drawing hundreds of hopefuls from across Nigeria and various church denominations. After rigorous online tests, the victorious few advanced to the eviction show, where they battled individually and in groups for the coveted 12 keys to the big house. Now, after weeks of challenges filled with endurance, knowledge, suspense, and action, we proudly present the final 12 stars who will vie for the title "Bible Personality of the Year." With the grand prize of 10 Million Naira and a brand new car.

  • The Big House Experience (Sneak Peek)

    The Big House is the heart of the Bible Stars Reality show, providing a 30-day housing experience for the 12 final stars, streamed for viewers' participation and voting.

    Pre-production to production spans 45 days: 30 days for housing and 15days for pre-camping to prepare and orientate the stars.

    Promising to set a new standard in television, the Big House boasts over 15 segments of pure and worthy entertainment, ensuring non-stop inspiration and excitement. From planned appearances of A-list celebrities ministering to the stars to mind-blowing intellectual challenges in "The Showroom" hosted by top Nigerian hosts, every moment is set to harmonize spiritual and entertaining elements while streaming on DSTV Channels for a worldwide audience.

    The proposed budget of the bible stars reality show is 300 Million Naira only. This amount will cater to the show-stopping arrangements and standard structure of this kingdom valued show. The vision is to showcase heaven on earth and its riches!

    Plans for the Big House Experience include:

    • 1. Appearance of Angels: We are making use of top celebrities to represent brands by ministering prizes to those who win prizes within the show and they will also minister with or minister to the stars.
    • 2. The Showroom: This segment of the big house experience is an avenue created for intellectual and spiritual challenges hosted by top Nigerian personalities. The stars will not only compete, they will have the opportunity to showcase God's wisdom and their perspective on different topics and heir approach to a kingdom lifestyle.
    • 3. The Lord's Garden: A part of the big house that is reserved as a scared place where the contestants will have a feel of a one-on-one relationship with God. It is an interactive area where Stars will go time to time to fellowship with God when they are called upon by "The voice in the garden" (another beautiful aspect of the show). The garden is a beautiful display of Kingdom royalty, depicting heaven on earth.
    • 4. Realms of Glory: This is a special aspect of the show which is designed to be a treasure house of heavenly blessings. In this part of the big house, the keys won by the housemates during the eviction show will be used to unlock the heavenly blessings stored in this Realms of Glory for themselves. An exhibition of God's favor, offering free and worthy gifts to the stars.
