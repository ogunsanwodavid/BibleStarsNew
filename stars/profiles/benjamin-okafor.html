BENJAMIN OKAFOR (Big Ben)

Benjamin Okafor, a member of Living Faith Church (Winners Chapel) from Delta State, has a variety of hobbies including sports, singing, writing, and teaching. With talents ranging from athletics to acting and oratory, Benjamin aspires to reach the pinnacle of his career.

In anticipation of the Big House, Benjamin expects to seize every opportunity within and outside its walls. If he wins the prize money, he plans to invest in self-improvement and initiatives to alleviate poverty in Nigeria. His favorite part of the Eviction Show was the Majestic Parade, while the most challenging aspect was the Targeted Lines segment.

Three presentations that left a lasting impact on Benjamin were those of Onyedika Nwosu, Babasanya Ademide, and Ogechukwu Gilbert. Their performances were captivating, inspiring, and deeply moving, respectively.
